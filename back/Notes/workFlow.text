End-to-End Workflow Summary
Teacher Registration:
    Teacher fills out the registration form, including salary and school ID.
    Backend creates a new Teacher instance.

Student Registration:
    Students register, providing necessary details and selecting their school.
    Backend creates new Student instances.

Teacher Logs In:
    Teacher logs in using their credentials.
    Backend returns a JWT token.

Teacher Creates Class:
    Teacher uses the token to authenticate and creates a new class.
    Backend associates the class with the teacher and school.

Teacher Assigns Students:
    Teacher selects students from their school to add to the class.
    Backend updates the many-to-many relationship between Class and Student.
    
Teacher Takes Attendance:
    Teacher selects the class and marks students as present or absent.
    Backend records attendance entries linked to the student, class, and teacher.
